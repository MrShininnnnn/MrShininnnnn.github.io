Shining Lab researches computational linguistics, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning, with a focus on multilingual systems and large language model evaluation — approached from the perspective of human cognition and how far neural networks can go toward systematic language understanding.

For research collaborations or opportunities, please feel free to get in touch.
