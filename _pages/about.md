Shining Lab researches computational linguistics, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning, with a focus on multilingual systems and large language model evaluation — approached from the perspective of human cognition and how far neural networks can go toward systematic language understanding.

For research collaborations or opportunities, please feel free to get in touch.

#### Members

**Ning Shi (石宁)** leads Shining Lab. He is a Ph.D. candidate in Computing Science at the University of Alberta, supervised by Prof. Grzegorz Kondrak and affiliated with the Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii). Before his doctoral studies, he worked as a Senior Algorithm Engineer at the Alibaba Artificial Intelligence Governance Laboratory (AAIG). He holds master's degrees from the Georgia Institute of Technology, Syracuse University, and New York University.
